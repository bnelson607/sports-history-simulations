2022 MLB Season

Season In Review

League Developments

  • Cleveland officially changed their name from the Indians to the Guardians
  • Regular season tiebreaker game eliminated
  • Playoffs expanded to 12 teams: Top two seeds in each league receive a bye, Wild Card Round is a best-of-3 with the higher seed hosting every game

Notable Team Performances

  • Dodgers won a franchise record 111 games - their third consecutive season with at least 106 wins - but were eliminated by the 89-win Padres in the NLDS
  • Mets had a 10.5-game lead on June 1, but lost the division to the Braves
  • Mariners qualified for the playoffs for the first time since 2001
