2020 NFL Season

Season In Review

Regular Season

League News & Notes

  • Due to the pandemic, all preseason games were cancelled, stadium capacities were significantly reduced, and 13 games were postponed or rescheduled.
  • The Washington Redskins retired their name and temporarily became the Washington Football Team.
  • The Oakland Raiders relocated to Las Vegas.
  • The playoffs expanded from 12 to 14 teams with the third wild card team in each conference facing the 2-seed; only the top seed in each conference would receive a bye to the Divisional Round.

Tiebreakers

  • Tennessee won the AFC South over Indianapolis by having a superior record within the division (5-1 vs. 4-2).
  • Baltimore earned the 5-seed over Cleveland by sweeping them during the regular season; they finished ahead of Indianapolis by winning the head-to-head matchup in Week 9.
  • Cleveland earned the 6-seed over Indianapolis based on their head-to-head victory in Week 5.
  • New Orleans earned the 2-seed over Seattle by virtue of having a better conference record (10-2 vs. 9-3).
  • Chicago earned the 7-seed over Arizona based on a having a better record in common games.
